Rockeseat's Explorer coursework

Repository for managing my projects and studies in Rockeseat's Explorer coursework.

Status: In Progress (50%)

This repository presents my solutions to the technical challenges I faced while training as a web developer in Rocketseat Explorer. You can learn more about Rocketseat itself and view the syllabus for this course by following this link. The repository you see is primarily intended to record my development into a full stack web developer!

The Explorer course is broken down into different levels of complexity and is designed for people who have no prior knowledge of technology. The initial phase covers the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then later builds into more advanced topics like Node.js, communicating with APIs, SQL, React, and more.

In the Important Projects section, I highlight the projects I have found most interesting, tricky, and challenging so far, while in the What's coming next? section, you'll learn what I'm currently studying and find a small overview of my journey in web development.

Well, that's it folks! Creating this website was really an enjoyable experience!
